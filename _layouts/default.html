About me

After graduating from Washington University in St. Louis, I spent some time in Chicago, IL doing improv comedy before eventually pursuing a degree in the visual arts.

It wasn't until I attended the Center for Digital Imaging Arts (CDIA) at Boston University that I discovered the world of coding. After a few late-night troubleshooting sessions, I was hooked.

My design philosophy involves creating unique and engaging experiences from the ground up. I find inspiration in minimalism, futuristic aesthetics, hip hop and electronic music, as well as nature and wildlife.
